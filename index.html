Whether you need someone to open doors, manage pipelines and convert leads, or build the marketing engine that keeps your pipeline full, I bring the expertise and energy to make it happen.

Business Development
I identify new revenue opportunities, build strategic partnerships, and create pathways for sustainable growth. From prospecting to deal closure, I work as an extension of your team to open doors that matter.
Sales Representative
Consultative, values-led selling that builds trust while driving results. I manage the full sales cycle — from initial contact through to a signed client — with honesty and diligence at every step. In my most recent role, I contributed to over $6 million in sales across 3 years.
Lead Management
No lead left behind. I qualify inbound enquiries, implement structured follow-up processes, and nurture prospects through the pipeline — ensuring your marketing investment converts into real bookings and revenue.
Client Relationships
Strong, lasting client relationships are the foundation of any great business. I build genuine connections, manage expectations with honesty, and ensure your clients feel valued long after the first transaction.
Marketing Specialist
From campaign conception to post-analysis, I manage tactical and acquisition marketing that delivers measurable results. Brand strategy, content, agency management, and budget optimisation — all handled with care.

A little about me

The person
behind the results

I'm Andrew, a British-born, Gold Coast-based sales and marketing professional who made Australia home permanently in 2019. With a background spanning the UK, USA, and Australia, I bring a broad perspective and a genuine passion for helping businesses grow.

My career has taken me from growing national fitness brands across the UK to building one of Australia's most recognised hair restoration businesses. What drives me isn't the deal, it's watching clients find the right solution and businesses reach their potential.

Outside of work, I'm a proud husband to Laura and father to three energetic kids. We love everything the Gold Coast has to offer: the beach, the community, and the lifestyle. I'm also passionate about football, sport in general and have competed in multiple marathons. I bring that same commitment and energy to everything I do professionally.
